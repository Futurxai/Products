2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the Service (host your content, process subscriptions)

3. Third Parties We Share With

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share it with advertisers.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember preferences and measure usage. You can disable cookies in your browser, but parts of the Service may not work.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your subscription is active and for up to 24 months after cancellation, after which it is deleted or anonymised. Payment records may be retained longer where required by tax law.

6. Public Pages

Pages you publish via Love Digitally are accessible to anyone with the link. Do not put information there that you would not want public.

7. Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by writing to our contact email. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children

The Service is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Security

We use industry-standard security measures (HTTPS, secure server-side processing, restricted database access). No system is 100% secure — please do not share sensitive personal information through shared pages.

We will update this page when we make material changes. Continued use after the update means you accept the revised policy.
