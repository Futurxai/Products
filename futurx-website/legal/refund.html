Refunds & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 5 May 2026

This policy explains how cancellations and refunds work for the Love Digitally Premium subscription (₹9 per month).

The short version: You can cancel anytime, and you will not be charged again. We do not offer refunds for the current month, but you keep access until the end of the period you've already paid for.

1. Cancelling Your Subscription

  • You can cancel your Premium subscription at any time by emailing us (see Contact) or via the cancellation link in any payment receipt.
  • Cancellation stops all future charges. The next billing cycle will not be billed.
  • You will continue to have Premium access until the end of the current billing period.
  • We process cancellation requests within 2 business days.

2. Refunds

We do not offer refunds for the current month's subscription once it has been charged. This is because:

  • The subscription is a low-value monthly recurring service (₹9/month);
  • You have full access to the Premium features for the entire paid period; and
  • Cancellation prevents any future charges from being made.

3. Exceptions — When We Will Issue a Refund

We will refund a payment in full if:

  • You were charged due to a technical error on our side (e.g. duplicate charge, charge after cancellation);
  • The Premium service was completely unavailable for a continuous period of more than 7 days within the billing cycle;
  • Required by law.

Refunds in these cases are processed within 5–10 business days to the original payment method.

4. Failed or Disputed Payments

If a recurring charge fails (insufficient funds, expired card, mandate revoked), Razorpay may retry the charge per its standard retry schedule. If the retries fail, your subscription will be paused. To restore Premium access, simply re-subscribe.

If you believe a charge is unauthorised, contact us immediately so we can investigate and reverse it if appropriate.

5. How to Request a Refund

Email us with your payment ID (the pay_xxxxx string from your receipt) and a brief description of the issue. We respond within 2 business days.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we change this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above and apply the new policy only to subscriptions that begin after the change.
