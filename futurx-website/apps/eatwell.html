EatWell

Eat healthy. The Indian way.

Nutrition and meal planning app built for India. Track your meals with a database of 50+ Indian recipes across South Indian, North Indian, Continental, and Chinese cuisines.

Download APK Try Web App

Features

🍲

Indian Food Database

50+ recipes — Idli, Dosa, Dal Makhani, Poha, and more. South Indian, North Indian, Continental, Chinese cuisines.

📈

Calorie Tracking

Log every meal. See daily calories, protein, carbs, and fat. BMI calculator included.

📅

Weekly Meal Plans

7-day meal planner with breakfast, lunch, snack, and dinner. Export to PDF.

🛒

Grocery List

Auto-generated shopping list from your meal plan. Categorized by ingredient type.

📷

Food Scanner

Camera-based food detection with healthier swap suggestions.

🌙

Dark Mode

Full dark theme with persistent toggle. Easy on the eyes.
